About Us

Ichiraku Ramen was founded with a passion for exquisite konoha cuisine and implecable service. Our story began with a dream to create a dining Experience where every detail matters


Growing up in the Konoha, we were summoned by the sights, smells and taste of tradtional filipino dishes. Our love for food and our culture led us to create a menu that showcases the best of Filipino cuisine. From the classic adobo to the sweet and spicy Sinigang, we have dishes that will transport your taste buds to the Philippines.


Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere along with the dishes crafter from the finest ingredients. We take pride in sourcing our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers to ensure that our dishes are always fresh and delicious. We also strive to provide the exceptional customer service to make sure every guest leaves with a smile on their face.


we are commited to preserving the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines and sharing it with thw world. Our menu is carefully crafted to showcase the diversity and complexity of filipino cuisine. From spicy kick of our dishes to sweetness of out desserts, we have something for everyone
